    When enabled, this server is reachable from anything that can hit your machine on the network. On a plain LAN that includes anyone on the same Wi-Fi; on a Tailnet, anyone signed into your tailnet.

    Connection

    Filled from a saved connection with the same provider.

    How many messages from the end of the conversation the dynamic-thalamus block (memories / graph / temporal) is injected at. Identity stays at the top (cacheable); dynamic stuff goes here. Smaller = better model attention, larger = more cache hits on long sessions.
    When on, the end of a session runs a short summary (cheapest connection) so the next session resumes mid-thought via the temporal context. One small extra generation per session boundary — turn off to save it.
    When on, your Familiar thinks on its own during idle periods — picking topics from accrued interest weights and writing real entries to its Ponderings tome (which it can later reference in chat). Cadence is conservative (30 min – 6 hr depending on tier) and only runs when interests have been accrued; fresh installs stay silent. Off skips the whole thing.
    Multiplies the base ponder interval. 1× = default cadence (30 min – 6 hr). 2× doubles every interval (1 hr – 12 hr). Use this to dial token cost down without disabling entirely. Only stretches, never speeds up.

    Save the fields above as a named connection. The first one is your primary; the rest are tried in order as fallbacks when a request fails or returns empty content.

      If the model returns no content, retry the same connection this many times before moving to the next fallback.

      Names

      Use {{user}} and {{char}} in prompts to insert these names. {{elapsedTime}} is the gap between the two most recent user messages in the chat history; {{timeSinceLastSession}} is the gap since the previous session ended.

      Injected as a final user message after the conversation history, right before the AI responds. Use for in-character direction or formatting instructions.

      Built-in tools always available: get_datetime, get_session_info

      Chat

      Summaries of past topics, injected as context when their keywords appear.

      Generate a plain-text report with system info, the recent in-app event log, and the last sent prompt summary. Paste into a bug report.

      Browse and edit the memories, identity files, and knowledge-graph entries the Familiar sees. Destructive ops auto-snapshot first.

      When set, reminders, check-ins, and outreach notices are also pushed to this webhook — so your Familiar can still reach you when no tab is open. The escalation countdown for trusted-contact outreach only starts once a check-in actually reaches you here.

      People your Familiar may reach out to about you when threat is severe and you've been quiet a long time. The Familiar can only contact someone listed here — and every outbound also lands in your chat outbox so you can see exactly what was sent. Leave empty to disable outreach entirely.

      How to make a Discord webhook: on the contact's side, in their Discord server's channel settings → Integrations → Webhooks → New Webhook → Copy URL. Paste it here.

      Manage the people in your Familiar's social safety net — trust categories, villagers with platform aliases, and locations.

      Inspect what Unruh is tracking — current interest weights, threat-detection state & audit history, and the Familiar's autonomous ponderings.
